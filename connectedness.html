WHAT IS A COMPOSITE INDICATOR?
In order to better understand how neighborhood environments contribute to resident well-being and social mobility, we created three custom indicators.
These indicators were developed by selecting and combining specific variables to create comprehensive measurements of three key dimensions of neighborhood
opportunity:economic opportunity,risk & vulnerability, and connectedness.
